The 25th of August 2019 Alipay’s Ant Manor asked in classroom: how many strings does an Erhu have? People were rewarded when getting the right answer. This peak is the highest reached among all Chinese traditional instruments search heats from 2011 to 2020.

Cai Yang of the Central National Folk Orchestra, has been playing Erhu on the webcast platform since January 2017. On average, it was watched by more than 20,000 people at a time, with the most being watched by 200,000 people online. In contrast to the 2,000-seat concert hall at the National Theatre, the live-streaming platform is relevant for the dissemination of traditional arts’ culture.
