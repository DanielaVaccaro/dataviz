The Shanghai National Orchestra's first multimedia music scene, "Gardenia Blossoms", is staged in May 2019 with liuqin, xiao, erhu, and pipa as the main instruments. The result is the creation of a new sound of folk music, both ethnic but strongly modern.

“The Pipa Player” is originally an ancient poem, on 17 May 2020 a pop singer readapted the song in a TV show.

3.
On the 15th of May 2018 an old lady lost her backpack on the train and anxiously claimed that it contained something very important, her Pipa. The policeman thought it was a valuable antique pipa, but the bag contained instead two bags of loquats. This misunderstanding is caused by the fact that loquat in Chinese has the same sound as Pipa.
